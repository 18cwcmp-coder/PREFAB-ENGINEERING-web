About

Prefab Engineering delivers advanced pipework fabrication for HVAC and Fire projects across Victoria, offering end-to-end services from pipework design through to manufacture.

Our facility features robotic plasma cutting and robotic welding, enabling high-volume production with certified weld quality. With over 10 years of experience in delivering modular pipework, ductwork, and plantroom equipment, we provide proven prefabrication solutions that reduce project risk, minimise onsite labour, and accelerate installation programs.

By combining prefabrication expertise with detailed design, structural, and seismic engineering, we ensure all work is fully certified and compliant to the highest standards. Clients benefit from fewer site clashes, faster project handover, and predictable project costs.

Production and assembly are carried out in a a controlled environment, ensuring consistent quality, improved safety outcomes, and minimal disruption on active construction sites.

We are ISO 9001 • 45001 • 14001 certified.
Located at 38 Enterprise Way, Sunshine West.

What We Do

  • Pipework for HVAC and Fire Services
  • Prefabricated Pump and Heat Exchanger Skids
  • Fan Assemblies and Ductwork Risers
  • Steel Plant Platforms and Prefab Plantrooms
  • Welding certification, pressure testing, crane lift studies
  • Digital drafting in Revit, AutoCAD, Navisworks, Revizto, 3ds Max

History

Established in 1997, our pipe shop has operated under OD Engineering through Footscray (Methan St), Port Melbourne, Laverton and now Sunshine West — evolving into a highly automated prefab facility.
